The standard

How every story earns its place

Misstating the law helps no one. Three rules govern everything we publish.

Primary-source verified

The actual opinion. The actual statute. The legislature's own site — plus a reputable outlet. Every claim dated, every quote checked. If we're uncertain, we don't publish.

Strictly non-partisan

We report what a ruling or law says and its practical effect — who it covers and when it takes effect. Never a side, never spin, never a pundit's take.

Plain English

No legalese. Legal terms get defined the moment they appear. You understand the change in about a minute — hook, what it says, who it affects, effective date.
